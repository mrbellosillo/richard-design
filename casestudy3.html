Driver Application Experience

Designed the “Apply as Driver” page for a transportation platform, focusing on simplifying the application process, reducing friction, and structuring detailed requirements into a user-friendly interface.

UI/UX Designer
DURATION

THE PROBLEM

The platform did not have a dedicated page for driver applications, making it unclear and difficult for potential drivers to apply. Also, driver requirements and onboarding details existed as long-form content, which could overwhelm users and reduce completion rates.

USERS NEED TO:
  • Apply quickly with minimal friction
  • Understand requirements without reading dense content
  • Know the steps involved before applying
  • Have alternative ways to apply (e.g., mobile app)
→ The main challenge was transforming detailed and structured requirements into a clear, scannable, and conversion-focused experience.
GOALS & OBJECTIVES
  • Create a clear and accessible driver application flow
  • Reduce friction by simplifying form inputs
  • Improve readability of requirements through structured content
  • Guide users toward completing the application
  • Maintain consistency with the existing design system
UNDERSTANDING THE WORKFLOW
  1. Reviewed driver onboarding requirements and documentation
  2. Identified friction points in long-form content
  3. Analyzed how users interact with forms and application pages
  4. Focused on reducing cognitive load and improving scannability
KEY INSIGHT
“Users are more likely to complete applications when information is simplified, structured, and easy to scan.”
PROCESS
1
Layout & Structure
Focused on organizing the form into clear sections to improve readability and reduce cognitive load. Information was grouped logically to guide users step-by-step through the application process.
2
Interaction Design
Designed input fields with clear labels, consistent spacing, and intuitive behavior to ensure a smooth and predictable form-filling experience.
3
Consistency
Maintained consistent alignment, spacing, and component usage across the form to create a cohesive and professional interface.
4
Refinement
Refined typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy to enhance clarity and ensure that important elements such as section titles and call-to-action buttons stand out.

RESULTS & IMPACT

REFLECTION

This project strengthened my ability to design conversion-focused interfaces while balancing detailed requirements with usability. It highlighted the importance of simplifying content and structuring information in a way that supports user decision-making without overwhelming them.

Designing this experience reinforced that clarity and focus are critical in form-based interfaces, where reducing friction directly impacts completion rates.
