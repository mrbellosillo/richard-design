DESIGN DIRECTION
Mood Board + UI Theme
OBJECTIVE
Establish a strong design direction using color theory, visual inspiration, and typography from a single prompt.
OVERVIEW

Established a complete design direction for a calm, nature-inspired meditation app using a monochromatic green color system. The deliverables included a Canva mood board with curated images and aesthetic cues, a color palette board, a typography board with Lato as the primary typeface, and a Figma mockup of sample UI components. The design direction conveyed a peaceful, zen, and calm aesthetic.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS
  • Developed a monochromatic green system with Deep Pine as CTA, Driftwood as neutral, and Warm Sand as accent
  • Selected Lato for its space efficiency and readability in mobile app interfaces
  • Designed UI components (buttons, cards, headers, badges) aligned to the mood board direction
  • Curated imagery conveying peaceful, zen, and calm aesthetics with consistent color palette

DESIGN LEAD FEEDBACK

The selected photos effectively convey a peaceful, zen, and calm aesthetic. Consistency in color selection is commendable. Deep Pine is recommended as CTA color; Warm Sand as primary accent for warmth in progress bars.

REFLECTION

This was the first task where I designed from brand direction down to a component. Seeing a mood board translate into actual UI components made design systems thinking real — not just theoretical.
