QR-Based Attendance Management System

Designed the admin-side interface of a QR-based attendance system that replaces manual paper time tracking. The focus was on structured data presentation, clarity, and consistent UI patterns to support efficient attendance management.

ROLE
UI/UX Designer
DURATION
1 Month
TOOL
Figma
Jump to final screens
THE PROBLEM

The company previously relied on manual, paper-based attendance tracking. This process was inefficient, difficult to monitor, and prone to data inconsistencies.

ADMINISTRATIVE USERS NEED TO:
  • Manage employee records securely
  • Generate and validate QR-based clock-ins
  • Monitor daily attendance in real time
  • Export structured attendance reports
→ The main challenge was designing a system that handled dense data while remaining easy to scan and operate.
GOALS & OBJECTIVES
  • Simplify navigation for frequent admin tasks
  • Provide clear visual feedback for system actions
  • Support exportable attendance reports (CSV, PDF, Excel)
  • Improve readability of attendance tables
  • Create a consistent layout and spacing system
  • Enable structured employee management
UNDERSTANDING THE WORKFLOW
  1. Observed how manual attendance was handled day-to-day
  2. Identified inefficiencies in record tracking and data retrieval
  3. Reviewed dashboard patterns for structured data-heavy systems
  4. Prioritized clarity and speed for admin-facing workflows
KEY INSIGHT
"Administrative interfaces must prioritize structure and usability over decoration."
PROCESS
1
Layout & Structure
Designed a clear and organized layout to present attendance data efficiently. The interface was structured to prioritize important information such as employee names, dates, and attendance status, making it easy to scan and manage records.
2
Interaction Design
Focused on creating intuitive interactions for navigating and managing attendance data. Actions such as viewing records and switching between sections were designed to be straightforward and predictable.
3
Consistency
Maintained consistent use of spacing, alignment, and components across the interface to ensure a cohesive and professional look throughout the system.
4
Refinement
Refined typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy to improve readability and enhance the overall user experience, ensuring that key information stands out clearly.

✔ Delivered a consistent and scalable admin interface

REFLECTION

This project strengthened my ability to design structured, data-heavy systems. It reinforced the importance of hierarchy, consistency, and clarity when designing administrative interfaces. Designing for internal tools taught me that efficiency and legibility matter far more than decoration — every layout decision had to justify itself through usability.
